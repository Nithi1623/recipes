Chicken Dum Briyani Recipe (2Nos)

By Nithyaraja

  1. Take a bowl and add 2 cup of rice
  2. Wash the rice with water and soak the rice with water and keep it seperately for 30 minutes
  3. Take a cooker or briyani cooking handi
  4. Add 2 tbsp of oil
  5. Add 1 cinamon stick, 1 briyani leaf, 4 cloves, 1 cardamon
  6. Add 2 chopped onion and saute until it turns deep brown
  7. Add 1 green chilli
  8. Add 2 tbsp of kashmiri chilli powder and saute for 2 minutes
  9. Add handfull of coriander and saute for 2 minutes
  10. Add handfull of mint leaf and saute for 2 minutes
  11. Add 1 tbsp of ginger garlic paste and saute for 2 minutes
  12. Add 1 chopped tomato and saute for 2 minutes
  13. Add 1/2 cup of curd and saute for 2 minutes
  14. Add 1/2 kg of chicken and saute for 5 minutes
  15. Add 4 cups of water for 2 cup of rice and boil for 5 minutes
  16. Add salt
  17. After boiling, add the rice to the cooker
  18. Heat dosa pan
  19. Close the cooker or Briyani handi with a plate
  20. Place some weight on the top of the plate
  21. place the cooker or briyani handi on the dosa pan
  22. Keep the flame in low and cook for 15 minutes
  23. After 15 minutes, open the cooker or briyani handi and serve the Chicken Dum Briyani
